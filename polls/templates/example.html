Let's talk about security questions

Some security questions are awful. Some don't have memorable answers. Some are easy for imposters to figure out - compromising your security.

The shortcomings of security questions will vary from person to person - yet the questions aren't selected by a diverse group of people. This study is an attempt to get feedback from a diverse audience, representative of a larger population of users.

The following page will have a list of security questions. For each security question, upvote if you think it's a good question and downvote if it isn't. Upvote other users' reasons for it being bad, and add your own.

Don't think of this as a survey, think of it as a discussion. You can learn about how other people feel, contribute as much as you like, and return later to add more.

Don't answer the questions, comment and vote what makes them good or bad
